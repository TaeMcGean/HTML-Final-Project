Total War Warhammer

A guide to larger-than-average units

Large monsters are cool, this is a simple fact of life that nobody can disprove. witnessing as a masterfully created army of highly trained warriors be bulldozed by an army of giant lizards will never not make me happy.
However, with so many factions to choose, how can you decide which giant hell monsters, murderous reptiles, or mechanical war machines to throw at your enemies?
On this site, i will attempt to go over the strengths and weaknesses about many of these magestic creatures, and which ones you should use in your worldwide conquests.

Please note: if a faction does not have any monstrous untis, it will not be listed.
