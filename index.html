A guide to Big Lizard Warfare

Large monsters are cool, this is a simple fact of life that nobody can disprove. witnessing as a masterfully created army of highly trained warriors be bulldozed by an army of giant lizards will never not make me happy.
On this site, i will attempt to go over the strengths and weaknesses about many of these sneaky snakes, combat crocodiles, and Supersized Saurus, and which ones you should use in your worldwide conquests.

Unit Value
